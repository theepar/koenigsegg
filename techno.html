Overview

Koenigsegg is truly tiers above its competition. Even with low-volume quantities, we develop and build several components of our powertrains rather than sourcing them from other manufacturers, and we have done so since our beginnings in 1994. Designing bespoke high-performance powertrains in-house gives us the freedom to develop record-breaking technology unrestricted by industry limitations or compromises.

Our ultimate-performance megacars are the proof of concepts of the incredible powertrain engineering at Koenigsegg.
